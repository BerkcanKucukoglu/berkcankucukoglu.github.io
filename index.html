About

My name is Berkcan Kucukoglu. I graduated in 2017 with a bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering and I am currently completing my master's at Isik University. My studies in civil engineering were heavily focused on designing highways and junctions, as well as research of public transportation opportunities. I am an expert in computer aided drawings, micro simulations, GIS and R&D. I am also passionate about applying 3D printings, UAV flights and AR&VR implementations to the problems in traffic engineering.

I want to make an impact by applying novel technologies to real-world problems. I am particularly interested in the applications of micro simulations to public transportation problems and highway projects. I am always looking for ways to improve my skills as an engineer and want to be on the cutting-edge of the transportation engineering industry.
